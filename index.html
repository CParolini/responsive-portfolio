About Me

First and foremost, my family is the most important thing in my life. My wife, Caroline, and I met in the 5th grade. We remained friends through our grade and high school careers, finally dating our junior year. We went to college together, got married afterward, moved to Austin and now have a beautiful 14 month old daughter, Elizabeth.

When I'm not spending time with my family, I'm really into outdoor activities and overall health. Austin has been the perfect place for a hobby like this. I take full advantage of everything it has to offer. My favorite outdoor activity is hiking. Whether that be in the flat nothingness that is west Texas or climbing to tallest peaks around.
